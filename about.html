About Me

Hello Everyone. My name is Nick Mazer and I am 25 years old. I started coding in college and didn't realize how much I actually enjoyed doing this until I started working a job with little creativity. Although it is troubling and sometimes really difficult, I really enjoy the challenges it gives me and the gratification I get when I accomplish them.

I wish this came more naturally! All good things don't come easy!

I am new to this so cut me some slack. I ran into mulitple problems while constructing this page. At first I was frustrated, but I realized that nothing would get done if I just gave up. Reading and researching helped me gain a better understanding of what is necessary to make a webpage actually work.

With the help of my great instructors Bj and Oggi this webpage went from an idea to a reality.

Thanks for taking the time to check out my first page. Better things are to come!
